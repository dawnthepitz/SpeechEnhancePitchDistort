Speech Enhance Pitch Distort

Enhances the Speech by reduction of the sound and Distorts the Pitch of the Speech to transform a male sound to female sound and vice versa

Requirements for the Application if you want to toy around the App:
Visual C# 2010 Express or Higher versions of Visual Studio with C#
MATLAB 2013a or higher version of MATLAB(May not grant full functionality especially on decrapated/removed functions of the latter version)

Configurations to make:
Please copy/move all of the .m files in the MATLAB Folder
Compile the C# App and have fun.
